Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password?" Follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

What should I do if I suspect fraud on my account?

If you suspect fraudulent activity, contact our support team immediately. We will investigate and take necessary action to secure your account.

How can I open a new account?

You can open a new account online by visiting our account creation page or by visiting your nearest branch.

Where can I find my account statement?

Your account statements are available in the "Statements" section of your online banking account.
