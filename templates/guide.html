Getting Started

Learn the basics of setting up and using online banking.

  1. Create your account on the bank's website.
  2. Verify your email and phone number.
  3. Log in with your credentials to access your account.

Security Tips

Follow these tips to keep your online banking experience safe.

  • Use a strong password and change it regularly.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Never share your login details.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

What should I do if I suspect fraud?

Contact your bank immediately and report any suspicious activity.
